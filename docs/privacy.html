⭐ Privacy Policy – Sticker Quest

Last updated: April 3, 2026

No Data Collection

Sticker Quest stores all data only on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Progress Sync

Progress data (points, task completion, sticker collection) is synced to our secure server to enable progress across devices. This data is associated with your Apple ID and can be deleted by contacting gyeningcorp@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

This app is designed for use by children ages 4–7 under direct parental supervision. We comply fully with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • No personal information is collected from children
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No advertising or tracking of any kind
  • No in-app chat or social features
  • No location tracking
  • No user-generated content uploads

Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device using browser localStorage:

  • Parent name (first name only)
  • Child nickname and avatar selection
  • Task completion history
  • Points and sticker collection
  • App settings and preferences

Data Deletion

To delete all data: Go to Grown-Ups → Settings → Reset Data. You can also clear your browser/app storage through your device settings.

In-App Purchases

Premium features are available as a one-time purchase through Apple In-App Purchases. No payment information is stored by this app. All billing is handled by Apple.

No Advertising

Sticker Quest contains zero advertisements. No ad networks, no behavioral tracking, no IDFA.

Contact

Questions? Email us at: gyeningcorp@gmail.com
