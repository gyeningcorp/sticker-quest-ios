⭐ Privacy Policy – Sticker Quest

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Sticker Quest is a chore and behavior chart app for children ages 4 and up, designed to be used by parents together with their kids. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights — in plain language.

Who Creates Accounts

Account creation is optional and is done by a parent or guardian using Sign in with Apple. Children do not create accounts. If a parent chooses not to sign in, the app works entirely offline with all data stored locally on the device — no account or server connection is required.

What Information We Collect

When a parent signs in with Apple, we may receive and store the following:

  • Apple ID (hashed): A one-way hashed identifier provided by Apple — we never see your actual Apple ID.
  • Name (optional): Only if the parent chooses to share it via Apple's sign-in flow.
  • Email address (optional): Only if the parent chooses to share it via Apple's sign-in flow. Apple may provide a private relay address.
  • Progress data: Points, level, completed tasks, unlocked stickers, and streak count.
No information is collected directly from children. All account-level data is associated with the parent's Apple account.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No location data
  • No microphone or camera access
  • No behavioral profiling or fingerprinting
  • No advertising identifiers (IDFA or similar)
  • No in-app chat or social features
  • No user-generated content uploads

Where Data Is Stored

When a parent is signed in, progress data is synced to our secure backend server hosted in the United States on Railway (railway.app), using a PostgreSQL database. Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

When a parent is not signed in, all data is stored locally on your device only and never leaves it.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Sticker Quest is designed for use by children ages 4+ under direct parental supervision and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • Accounts are created by parents, not children
  • No personal information is collected directly from children
  • No data is sold or shared with third parties
  • No advertising or behavioral tracking of any kind
  • No third-party analytics SDKs
  • No location tracking
  • No social or chat features

Parents have full control over account creation, data, and deletion at all times.

How We Use Your Data

Data we collect is used solely to:

  • Sync progress across devices when a parent is signed in
  • Restore progress if the app is reinstalled

We do not use data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. The only external service involved in data handling is our Railway/PostgreSQL backend, which stores progress data on our behalf.

Data Deletion

To delete all local data on your device: go to Grown-Ups → Settings → Reset Data.

To request deletion of any data stored on our server (including your hashed Apple ID and progress data), email us at gyeningcorp@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request." We will process your request within 30 days.

In-App Purchases

Premium features are available as a one-time purchase through Apple In-App Purchases. No payment information is stored by this app. All billing is handled entirely by Apple.

No Advertising

Sticker Quest contains zero advertisements. There are no ad networks, no behavioral tracking, and no advertising SDKs of any kind.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions or requests? Email us at: gyeningcorp@gmail.com
